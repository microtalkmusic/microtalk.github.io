Micro Talk is the playground of composer duo Skipta and Nana. The former is known for creating unique sonic landscapes and the latter for her soulful song crafting. Together they join forces in their studio in Amsterdam to write and record tailor made compositions. They worked on art projects such as Dune Reworked, which will be shown at De School, and commercials for companies as Talisman and Hatch. Micro Talk will release an EP in 2019.
